The Code of Ethical Honor of the Alexandria Higher Institute for Engineering and Technology

  • Charter objective
    The Alexandria Higher Institute for Engineering and Technology in Smouha adopts a policy of the highest degree of honesty, integrity, and justice. The institute considers this policy the responsibility of all members of the institute. The members of the board of directors, the dean of the institute, his deputy, heads of scientific departments, members of the teaching staff, the supporting staff, administrators, and students must adhere to what was stated in this charter and cooperate to establish the principles of honesty, honesty, integrity, sincerity, and all the elements that maintain the reputation of the institute towards society. The purpose of drawing up this charter is not to specify responsibilities and work method, but rather the goal is to establish a moral code of honor that is known to all members of the institute and a binding reference for them in their work and in dealing with others, whether from inside or outside the institute. This charter contains a set of general responsibilities and obligations for the institute community. It also includes the specific ethical responsibilities and obligations of each class separately.
  • Application
    Staff, the workers, and the students at the institute.
    First: Obligations of all members of the Alexandria Higher Institute of Engineering and Technology
    Obligations to others
    All members of the Alexandria Higher Institute for Engineering and Technology in Smouha acknowledge the following obligations:
    A- To the students:
    1. Considering justice and respect for the student's personality, granting him personal freedom in expressing his opinion, and not discriminating between students on the grounds of creed, color, gender, or kinship.
    2. Providing a university environment free of injustice, exhaustion, discrimination, and threats.
    3. Taking all measures to ensure that the student obtains the highest educational and cultural level.
    4. That everyone is a good example for the student, each in his place.
    b- To the faculty members:
    1- Reverence and respect for those in charge of teaching and considering what they do as a sacred act.
    2- Provide the fullest conditions for them to carry out their teaching and research duties, serve the community to the fullest, and grant them the necessary academic freedom to present their ideas.
    C- Towards Academic Administration:
    1- The academic administration consists of the dean of the institute, his deputy, and heads of departments.
    2- Appropriate conditions must be provided for the members of the academic administration to work with the highest possible efficiency and in a safe environment that is not tainted by any illegal or immoral behavior.
    D- To the Board of Directors:
    1- Dealing with the Board of Directors with all sincerity and honesty and not conveying any false or untruthful information to it.
    2- Managing the Institute's property, including buildings, equipment, and so on, with the highest possible wisdom and reason, according to the directives of the Board of Directors and the Dean's instructions, and within the limits of the law.
    3 - Implementation of the general strategy of the Institute, which is set by the Board of Directors with all honesty and sincerity.
    E - towards the neighbor:
    The Commitment of everyone to work in the Institute and to use everything in the Institute in a manner that does not harm the surrounding environment, including individuals and public and private property, whether the damage is material or moral.
    F- Towards the official authorities:
    Always adhere to honesty and honesty in all the Institute's dealings with official agencies and authorities, whether at the governorate level, ministries, or official government agencies.
    Obligations towards the Institute's resources
    All members of the Alexandria Higher Institute of Engineering and Technology in Smouha undertake to comply with the following standards in their behavior and actions and to avoid any behavior or behavior that exceeds these standards:
    1. Not to use the Institute's resources except for the purposes for which they are intended. Commitment to managing those resources as wisely, honestly, and adequately as possible and not using them for personal benefit or the benefit of any other person.
    2. Not to provide any material or moral resource from the Institute's resources to any person or entity to achieve an unfair personal benefit.
    Commitments to the basics of the relationship with suppliers:
    1- Suppliers mean the category of contractors, maintenance and repair workers, and everyone who deals with the institute to obtain funds.
    2- It is not permissible for any member of the institute to approve or assign any work to a supplier if there is any relationship between this member and the supplier, or any material or functional transactions of any kind and at any level unless he discloses this relationship to the dean of the institute in writing with an explanation The reason for dealing with this resource.
    3- The need for the institute's members to make sure that their dealings with suppliers are in the interest of the institute and do not bring any foreseeable losses, and that the suppliers chosen for dealing are the best choice.
    4- It is strictly prohibited for the members of the Institute and their first-degree relatives (children and parents) and second-degree (brothers, sisters, grandparents, and grandchildren) and their spouses to accept any in-kind or material gifts or sums of money or to achieve any personal benefit from suppliers, and this prohibition applies for a period time that extends for one year from before The suppliers start dealing with the Institute, during dealing with the Institute, and for one year after dealing with the Institute.
    Obligations towards dealing with gifts presented to employees and gifts presented to the institute:
    1- It is not permissible for any member of the academic administration or of the teaching, assistant, and administrative bodies or the workers of the institute to accept or solicit any gift or any bid of material value or obtain a moral service that results in influencing a decision about off on the institute.
    2- This does not preclude the acceptance of symbolic gifts of low material value on some occasions such as holidays and celebrations with transparency.
    3- Gifts of high material value may be accepted if they are presented to the institute itself and not to a specific person, and this gift is kept in the institute, and it is not permissible to use it personally.
    Commitment to the confidentiality of accounts and documents:
    Accounts and documents that are confidential and directly affect the interest of the Institute may not be circulated or viewed except for those who have the right to do so by a written decision or according to the job description. All members of the Institute undertake to abide by this rule. academic examination work, such as question papers, control work, and financial affairs that the Institute's interest requires to maintain confidentiality, falls.
    Second: Behavior and behavior
    A- Students:
    1- Full commitment not to violate any of the provisions of the charter.
    2- Reverence and full respect for the members of the teaching staff and the supporting staff at all levels, respect for the members of the administrative staff and the workers and help them in carrying out the tasks entrusted to them.
    3- Not to take any action that affects academic integrity, such as cheating in exams, quoting, plagiarizing the works of others, and lying in all its forms.
    4- Complete preservation of the Institute's buildings and equipment.
    5- Considering the virtues of morals, etiquette, and honesty in dealing with colleagues.
    B- Members of the teaching staff and the supporting staff
    1- Ensuring respect, justice, personal dignity, and effective treatment for all students without discrimination.
    2- Commitment to providing the best possible educational service to students.
    3- Paying attention to self-development and raising the scientific and academic level through constant access to the latest research and developments in the field of specialization, which is reflected in the educational process and the entire institute.
    4- Full and permanent commitment to academic integrity standards.
    5- Mutual respect between colleagues and academic and administrative departments at the Institute.
    C- Academic Administration:
    1- Exerting maximum effort to promote the institute and raise its level among other institutes.
    2- Work to provide a suitable environment for all individuals belonging to the institute.
    3- The administration of the institute deals in a democratic manner that is characterized by firmness and justice while avoiding individual and emotional decisions.
    4- Establishing complete justice among members of the university community in providing opportunity, and avoiding persecution because of creed, color, gender, or difference in performance, and that the evaluation of individuals is objective and based on the consideration of reasons.
    5- Providing advice and advice to the Board of Directors of the Institute by following honesty, honesty, and sincerity.
    6- Serious and permanent work to establish and document the Institute's relationship with local community parties and foreign and international bodies and universities.
    Third: Professional ethics in teaching
    Teaching staff members and members of the supporting staff in carrying out teaching tasks must adhere to the following:
    ✅ Ensure that he masters the scientific subject that he is entrusted with teaching or qualifies himself for before accepting to teach it.
    ✅ Good preparation for his subject, with adequate awareness of its developments and developments, so that he can master the subject to the extent that qualifies him to teach it in the best way.
    ✅ Commitment to formal or informal quality standards in determining the scientific level of the subject he teaches, so that it is not higher than what is required, which creates unjustified difficulties, or it is easier than what is required, so it negatively affects the subsequent learning process, the level of the graduate, and the level of performance of professions in the field. community in the end.
    ✅ Commitment to creating opportunities for his students to achieve the highest level of achievement that their abilities allow.
    ✅ To announce to his students the framework of the course, its objectives, its contents, methods of evaluation and review, and its connection to the study program as a whole and accept students' discussion of all of this.
    ✅ To be committed to using teaching time well and in the interest of students, the institute, and society.
    ✅ To develop the student's logical thinking abilities and accept his reaching independent results based on this thinking.
    ✅ To respect the student's ability to think and encourage him to think independently.
    ✅ To allow discussion and objection by following the principles of constructive dialogue and by Following the accepted etiquette of hadith.
    ✅ To master the skill of teaching, and to use the methods and means that help him master teaching and make it interesting, enjoyable, and useful at the same time.
    ✅ To perform his work in the lecture, laboratory, studio, etc. with honesty and sincerity, keen on the cognitive and moral growth of his students and assistants.
    ✅ To fully follow up on the performance of his students possible, and to make available the results of the follow-up to his students and stakeholders to act accordingly
    ✅ To be a model of democratic values in freedom of thought, freedom of opinion, freedom of expression, and equality, and to seek to develop these values in its students.
    ✅ To give his students proper guidance regarding sources of knowledge, information containers, and study references.
    ✅ To consider, whenever possible, the transfer of an increasing burden of learning responsibility to the student by following appropriate teaching methods.
    ✅ Refrain from giving private lessons under any name, with or without pay.
    Professional ethics practices for engineers
    1- Engineers are looking to increase the effectiveness of their profession, by participating in engineering societies, learning from scientific schools, viewing engineering publications, and exchanging information and experience with fellow engineers. This is in addition to the fact that the engineer should not attract praise for himself, and he should avoid everything that would harm the reputation and honor of the profession.
    2- After graduating from the Alexandria Higher Institute of Engineering and Technology and belonging to the Egyptian Engineers Syndicate, the engineer must recite the following oath:
    I swear by Almighty God to perform my work with honesty and honor, to preserve the secret of the profession, and to respect its laws and traditions.
    To oblige him with all the honesty of the profession.
    Code of Honor for the Engineering Profession
    The engineer must abide by the code of ethics of the profession, which is an ethical code for engineers to advance the profession. Several meetings were held for the unions of engineers in the world to develop a charter of honor for the engineering profession. These meetings concluded with an international conference of engineers held in New York recently. This conference issued a charter of honor for the profession, which is as follows: 2- The engineer, after graduating from the Alexandria Higher Institute of Engineering and Technology and belonging to the Egyptian Engineers Syndicate, must recite the following oath:
    ✅ The engineer recognizes the principles of honesty, justice, and mutual respect that guide his actions in life
    ✅ An engineer's practices of his profession according to ethical principles and laws based on transparency and credibility.
    ✅ Performing the duties of his profession with sincerity to the public and his clients, and with integrity and uprightness to all.
    ✅ The engineer's concern for the common good, and willingness to use his talents and confidential information for the benefit of humankind.
    ✅ Enhancing the engineer's honor and dignity.
    ✅ The engineer avoided participating in projects of questionable qualities.
    ✅ The engineer treats his fellow engineers with tolerance and justice.
    Engineering performance standards
    The engineer must adhere to the standards of performing the engineering profession to preserve its dignity and safety in its implementation, which is as follows:
    ✅ Sincerity committed to integrity and justice, in proportion to the responsibilities entrusted to him, for a better life that provides humans with the means of ease and well-being.
    ✅ Competition is within a serious and honorable scope, based on competence, its basis on quality, the pillar is a profession, and distancing from illegal means of access and promotion such as mediation and bribery. The impartial assessment of human effort is proper and fair, and rights are put in the proper sense, whether they are material or moral.
    ✅ Public frankness in comfortable dealings, with constant steadfastness on scientific opinion, even if it is not in the personal interest, honesty, tolerance, and keeping secrets, wherever and whenever the good of work requires that.
    ✅ Acknowledgment of opinions in clarity and confidence, while not delving into unstudied topics or exposure to opinions in areas whose data is not available to him.
    ✅ Refrain from improvising engineering work and avoid emotional drift to non-scientific fields under psychological pressure or personal desires.
    ✅ The categorical refusal to share scientific information in illegal or destructive actions that may harm humans. And he must hand over those responsible for this to justice and put them in confrontation with the law.
    ✅ Taking pride in the title of "engineer" and preserving the high status of the profession, by not spreading rumors and slander against a colleague out of hatred and jealousy. As well as not covering up a colleague who is proven to have committed something that defames his title.
    ✅ Active participation in the development of the profession, and its enrichment with the fruit of continuous and uninterrupted effort, a contribution to the tremendous technological edifice of humanity.
    The engineer's relationship with the public
    The engineer must deal with the public in harmony, adaptation, and sound and honest judgment so that his relationship with his audience is as follows:
    ✅ Seek to educate the public with engineering information and knowledge, and discourage the spread of false, suspicious, and exaggerated statements related to engineering.
    ✅ Adequate attention to the health and safety of the public who may be affected by the actions for which he is responsible. Expressing an opinion based on sufficient knowledge and sincere belief, when presenting it to testify before the judiciary, the prosecution, or the official authorities.
    ✅ Refrain from issuing tendentious statements, personal criticisms, or fake arguments, related to public policy, in favor of private parties that instructed him to do so, or hired him for them, unless he announces the party for which he works.
    ✅ Refrain from giving his opinion publicly on an engineering subject, unless he has sufficient information, and he has seen the facts related to the subject.
    Engineer's relationship with clients
    The engineer must deal with clients with honesty, non-discrimination, and the ability to work continuously, so that his relationship with his clients is as follows:
    ✅ Dealing with engineering matters about each client.
    ✅ Act with integrity with clients when supervising the implementation of contracts.
    ✅ To clarify his situation to his clients before starting his work or being associated with a specific business.
    ✅ Precaution for dangerous situations and events that determine life in the actions for which he is responsible or not responsible, and he must immediately direct the attention of those responsible directly to these cases and events.
    ✅ Presentation with complete frankness of the consequences that are expected to occur because of any proposed modification regarding an engineering work.
    ✅ Advising his client to use specialists, and cooperating with those specialists when he finds that the interest of his clients requires these services.
    ✅ Refrain from disclosing information related to his client's commercial or technical work without their consent.
    ✅ Refrain from accepting fees or financial rewards from more than one source for the same service or services related to the same work, without the consent of all stakeholders in those businesses.
    ✅ Refrain from accepting a material commission, direct grant, or intermediary from contractors or other groups.
    ✅ Refraining from interfering, from a financial point of view, with tenders between contractors in a work that he specializes in as an engineer unless his client allows him to do so.
    ✅ Immediate declaration to his client of any interest he has in a business that competes or affects his client's business, and he does not allow any interest he has in any business to affect his opinion of any engineering work he does, or he can be called to do as an employee.
    The engineer's relationship with colleagues
    The engineer must deal with fellow engineers with courtesy, courtesy, and self-control so that his relationship with his colleagues is as follows:
    ✅ Promoting the principle of adequate and decent remuneration for those who carry out engineering work, including those who carry out legal work, as this principle agrees with the public interest, and maintains the level of the profession.
    ✅ Diligence in finding opportunities to promote the engineers at their service and for their technical progress.
    ✅ Refrain from harming the technical reputation of any other engineer or his efforts. But if he believes that the work of some engineers is illegal and unfair, he must submit the information to the competent authorities for the necessary action.
    ✅Refrain from seeking to replace him with another engineer, if he knows that the necessary measures have been taken to employ that engineer.
    ✅ Refrain from competing with another engineer to obtain work by reducing his regular fees, especially after reviewing the conditions of another engineer.
    ✅ Refrain from exploiting his position as an employee with a certain salary to compete, unfairly, with another engineer in his work.
    ✅ Refrain from participating in the responsibility of doing a job with engineers whose principles do not conform to ethical foundations.
