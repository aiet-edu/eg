Vision and Mission of the Scientific Research Unit

In the interest of the Institute to follow the rules of programs to ensure the quality of the educational process and support scientific research, the Scientific Research Unit was established in 2018 by a decision of the Board of Directors of the Institute

process and support scientific research, the Scientific Research Unit was established in 2018 by a decision of the Board of Directors of the Institute

The unit has a clear vision of providing a stimulating and supportive environment to carry out scientific research and disseminate applied research thought by providing opportunities to participate in international conferences and publish in international scientific journals, as well as funded research projects.

The unit's mission is to contribute to solving community problems through the implementation of scientific research, activities, events, and scientific conferences that support community service considering the strategic plan.

The unit also adopts student creative and research centers to provide a stimulating environment for students and create a spirit of constructive technical creativity, in addition to creating a spirit of competitiveness and contact with graduates of other local and international institutes and colleges through participation in distinguished graduation projects in international and local competitions.
