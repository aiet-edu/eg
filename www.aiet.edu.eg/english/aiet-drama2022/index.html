The annual theatrical show for the academic year 2021-2022 A Puck for Every Citizen

Under the auspices of Prof. Dr. Mustafa Nasr, Dean of the Alexandria Higher Institute for Engineering and Technology, on Saturday, corresponding to 12/25/2021, the annual theatrical show of the institute was held, entitled A Puck for Every Citizen. Hisham Mohamed Abdel Hamid Younes, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Inspecta Middle East Company, Eng. Magdy Abdel Wadoud, Former Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Egyptian Petrochemical Company, Eng. Mohamed Ibrahim Yaqout, General Manager of Alexandria Petroleum Company previously. The show won the encouragement of all the attendees, The show was presented by the theater team of the Alexandria Higher Institute for Engineering and Technology and directed by / Ramy Nader
